Referral partner agreement

This page explains how the FineAppCraft referral program works — no need to message us to check the terms, they're all here.

How it works

You get a unique referral link (fineappcraft.com/?ref=YOURCODE). Any customer who completes a purchase through that link is tagged as your referral automatically. You never set or negotiate prices yourself — customers get the same published pricing and negotiation range as anyone else. Your commission is calculated automatically once a sale is paid.

Commission tiers

There are three tiers, capped at a maximum of 10 marketers total:

30%
up to 3 marketers
20%
up to 3 marketers
10%
up to 4 marketers
Important: commission is calculated on the template's published suggested price, not necessarily the final amount a customer pays. If a customer negotiates or pays above the suggested price, that's still fully collected by FineAppCraft — your commission doesn't increase past the suggested-price baseline. This keeps commission fair and consistent no matter what a customer ends up paying.

Staying active

  • 3-month activity requirement: if you haven't referred at least one completed (paid) sale within a rolling 3-month period, your referral access is paused and your slot may be given to someone else. You're welcome to reapply once you're ready to actively refer again.
  • Commission is only paid on completed, paid sales — not on quotes or requests that don't convert.
  • Your tier and referral code are personal to you and shouldn't be shared, resold, or transferred to anyone else.

Expectations

  • Represent FineAppCraft honestly — don't make promises about pricing, features, or timelines that aren't part of the published site.
  • No spam, misleading advertising, or impersonation of FineAppCraft in your marketing.
  • FineAppCraft reserves the right to adjust tiers, caps, or this agreement going forward — you'll be notified of any changes that affect your existing tier.
