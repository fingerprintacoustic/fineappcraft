Terms & conditions

Read this before requesting a quote or custom build below — you'll need to agree before either can be submitted.

1. Quotes & negotiation

Prices shown are starting ranges per template category. Offers within the shown range are accepted automatically. Offers below the range are reviewed manually and may be countered.

2. Free trial

A 7-day free trial is available with no payment required to start. If you continue using the app after the trial ends, payment at the agreed price becomes due. Access is paused after 7 days until payment is made.

3. Payment

You can pay in full, or pay a 30% deposit to get started with the remaining 70% due before your app goes live. All payments are processed by Stripe, PayPal, or Square; FineAppCraft does not store card details.

4. Revisions

Each project includes two rounds of minor revisions. Additional revisions or scope changes are quoted separately.

5. Ownership

Full ownership of the delivered app and its source code transfers to the customer once payment is received in full.

6. Refunds

The 30% deposit is non-refundable once work has started. Full refunds are available only if work has not yet begun. If paying in full upfront, the same 30% portion is treated as non-refundable once work starts, with the remaining 70% refundable only before work begins.

7. Delivery & support

Delivery timelines are estimates and communicated per project. 14 days of post-delivery bug-fix support is included at no extra cost.
